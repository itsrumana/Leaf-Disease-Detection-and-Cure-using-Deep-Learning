Cause:

• Isariopsis is a genus of fungi in the family Mycosphaerellaceae.The plant disease called isariopsis leaf spot is actually caused by Pseudocercospora vitis, formerly known as I.vitis

Symptoms:

• On leaf surface we will see lesions which are irregularly shaped (2 to 25 mm in diameter).
• Initially lesions are dull red to brown in color turn black later. If disease is severe this lesions may coalesce.

Treatment:

• Fungicides sprayed for other diseases in the season may help to reduce this disease.
• Fungicides for the management of Isariopsis leaf spot (Isariopsis indica var. zizyphi) of ber (Ziziphus mauritiana) cv. 'Gola'.
•The spray of fungicides at 10 l/plant was scheduled just after initiation of the disease under field condition.
•The fungicides used were mancozeb at 0.2%, carbendazim at 0.1%, copper oxychloride at 0.2%, propiconazole 25EC at 0.1%, difenconazole 10WP at 0.1% and neem oil at 3.0%.
